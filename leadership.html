02 — Leadership

Command Structure

Space Born operates on a structured chain of command designed to ensure fairness, efficiency, and long-term stability. Every level has a defined role, and every decision follows a clear system of accountability.

High Council 4 Members · Tier 1 Command

The High Council represents the core leadership of the kingdom. They set long-term vision, oversee all departments and major decisions, and maintain balance, fairness, and stability across the kingdom.

Responsibilities

Setting long-term vision and direction for the kingdom

Overseeing all departments and major decisions

Maintaining balance, fairness, and stability across the kingdom

Authority

⚖️

Holds veto power over any decision

🛡️

Can stop or delay actions deemed harmful or unnecessary

🌌

Acts as the final safeguard for the kingdom's future

High Council members — appointments pending

Low Council 9 Members · Specialized Leadership

The Low Council consists of specialized leaders, each responsible for a key area of the kingdom. They manage their respective departments, execute plans, maintain day-to-day operations, and coordinate with other departments and leadership.

Responsibilities

Managing their respective departments

Executing plans and maintaining day-to-day operations

Coordinating with other departments and leadership

Low Council positions — departments being organized

Governance

Decision System

To ensure decisions are not made impulsively, Space Born follows a two-tier voting process.

Voting Requirements

6/9

Low Council votes required to initiate any major action

3/4

High Council approval required for confirmation

VETO

High Council retains the right to veto or halt any decision

This system ensures:

No single person controls the kingdom
Every action is reviewed, discussed, and justified
Stability over chaos

Execution Layer

Alliance Leadership

R5s and Officers bridge the gap between kingdom command and frontline execution.

R5 Leadership

Main & Support Alliances — R5s are responsible for alliance-level execution.

  • Implement kingdom directives within alliances
  • Manage alliance activity, coordination, and discipline
  • Act as the bridge between leadership and members

Officers

Officers support R5s and departments in daily operations.

  • Assist with coordination, communication, and enforcement
  • Help maintain activity and organization within alliances
  • Ensure members stay aligned with kingdom goals
