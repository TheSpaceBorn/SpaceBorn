Recruitment Criteria

Who We're Looking For

Space Born is for dedicated players who want to be part of something real.

We Want

  • Active players who log in daily during growth and KvK
  • Players who follow strategy and respond to war calls
  • Team-first mentality — no lone wolves
  • Experienced RoK players who understand the game
  • Discord-active members who stay informed

Not a Fit If

  • You hit kingdom members without cause
  • You ignore war calls or go inactive during KvK
  • You leak kingdom information to outsiders
  • You're here to cause drama, not contribute
  • You refuse to follow the kingdom structure
