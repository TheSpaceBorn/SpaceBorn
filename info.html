Who We Are

About Space Born

Space Born is not a random project built on hype. It is led by experienced former kings, officers, and kingdom builders who have already been through the full Rise of Kingdoms cycle — from early-game growth to high-level KvK and long-term kingdom management.

We've seen what works. We've also seen what destroys kingdoms — poor leadership, favoritism, lack of structure, and short-term thinking.

This project exists because we wanted to build something better. Our goal with Space Born is simple: to create a well-structured, competitive, and stable kingdom where players are not just used — but developed, organized, and valued.

We are not here to experiment blindly. Every decision — from alliance structure to KvK planning — is based on experience, coordination, and clear long-term direction.

Why We're Here

Our Motivation

Most kingdoms fail not because of strength — but because of mismanagement and ego.

We built Space Born to change that.

A place where discipline matters more than noise.

Where effort is recognized, not ignored.

Where leadership is active, accountable, and experienced.

And where every member understands their role in the bigger picture.

We're not chasing unrealistic promises. We're building something sustainable, competitive, and respected.

Our Approach

What Makes Us Different

We don't believe in chaos or "figure it out later" leadership. Space Born runs on structure, planning, and clarity.

How We Operate

  • Clear kingdom direction from Day 1
  • Organized alliance hierarchy and responsibilities
  • Focus on KvK readiness, not just early power spikes
  • Strong communication between leadership and members

What We Build

  • A place where players grow together
  • A kingdom where loyalty actually matters
  • A system where success is shared across the kingdom
  • A community that feels like more than just orders and war

Space Born is more than just a restart. It's a project built by people who understand the game — and are ready to do it properly this time.

Kingdom Laws

Rules & Protocols

Breaking these laws has consequences. Leadership enforces them without exception.

Combat Laws

Zero tolerance for hitting kingdom members without official war declaration.

R4/R5 rally participation is mandatory during KvK season.

All diplomacy requests must go through official channels only — never DM enemies directly.

Tile hitting allies is a bannable offense during active KvK.

City burning of allies results in immediate removal from the kingdom.

Discord & Communication

Discord is mandatory — critical info and war calls are posted there only.

Mark yourself with your IGN + alliance tag in the Discord server.

War calls in the war-pings channel require immediate acknowledgement.

Leaking kingdom information to enemies will result in permanent removal.

Growth & Resources

New players follow the kingdom-approved build guide during the growth phase.

Resource sharing protocols will be distributed via Discord.

Kingdom pass purchase requirements will be outlined per alliance tier.

Rune usage and kingdom tech contribution requirements posted pre-KvK.

Activity & Commitment

Minimum activity requirements vary by alliance tier — posted in Discord.

Extended inactivity without notice may result in demotion or removal.

Alliance contribution to kingdom events is tracked and expected.

Leadership reviews performance regularly to maintain kingdom quality.
