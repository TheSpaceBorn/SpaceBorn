06 — Pilots

Verified Pilots

Trusted kingdom members available to pilot your account for a fee. All pilots are verified by Space Born leadership and are fully accountable within the kingdom.

What Is a Pilot?

Piloting Services

A pilot is a trusted, experienced player who temporarily plays your account on your behalf.

Common Services

  • KvK march management & rally joining
  • Farm account management (resource gathering)
  • Event completion & daily quest grinding
  • Scout runs and field operations
  • Pass opening & resource distribution
  • Custom agreements with pilot

Important Rules

  • Only use verified pilots from this list
  • Agree on scope and price before handing over access
  • Pilots are not allowed to make purchases or trade away items without permission
  • Disputes go through Space Born leadership
  • Unverified piloting services will not be protected by leadership
