Who We Are

About Space Born

Space Born is not a random project built on hype. It is led by experienced former kings, officers, and kingdom builders who have already been through the full Rise of Kingdoms cycle — from early-game growth to high-level KvK and long-term kingdom management.

We've seen what works. We've also seen what destroys kingdoms — poor leadership, favoritism, lack of structure, and short-term thinking. This project exists because we wanted to build something better.

Our goal: a well-structured, competitive, and stable kingdom where players are not just used — but developed, organized, and valued.

Why We're Here

Our Motivation

Most kingdoms fail not because of strength — but because of mismanagement and ego.

We built Space Born to change that. A place where discipline matters more than noise. Where effort is recognized, not ignored. Where leadership is active, accountable, and experienced.

And where every member understands their role in the bigger picture.

We're not chasing unrealistic promises. We're building something sustainable, competitive, and respected.

We don't believe in "figure it out later" leadership. Space Born runs on structure, planning, and clarity.

Kingdom Laws

Rules & Protocols

These laws are enforced without exception by leadership.

Combat Laws

Zero tolerance for hitting kingdom members without official war declaration.

R4/R5 rally participation is mandatory during KvK season.

All diplomacy through official channels only — never DM enemies directly.

Tile hitting allies is a bannable offense during active KvK.

City burning of allies results in immediate removal.

Discord & Communication

Discord is mandatory — all critical info and war calls posted there only.

Mark yourself with your IGN + alliance tag in the Discord server.

War calls require immediate acknowledgement.

Leaking kingdom information results in permanent removal.

Growth & Resources

New players follow the kingdom-approved build guide during growth phase.

Resource sharing protocols distributed via Discord.

Kingdom pass requirements outlined per alliance tier.

Rune usage and tech contribution requirements posted pre-KvK.

Activity & Commitment

Minimum activity requirements vary by tier — posted in Discord.

Extended inactivity without notice may result in demotion or removal.

Alliance contribution to kingdom events is tracked and expected.

Leadership reviews performance regularly to maintain kingdom quality.
