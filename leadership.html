02 — Leadership

Command Structure

A structured chain of command designed to ensure fairness, efficiency, and long-term stability. Every level has a defined role — every decision follows a clear system of accountability.

High Council 4 Members · Tier 1 Command

The High Council represents the core leadership of the kingdom. They set long-term vision, oversee all departments and major decisions, and maintain balance, fairness, and stability.

Authority

⚖️

Holds veto power over any decision

🛡️

Can stop or delay actions deemed harmful or unnecessary

🌌

Acts as the final safeguard for the kingdom's future

Low Council 9 Members · Specialized Departments

Specialized leaders each responsible for a key area of the kingdom. They manage departments, execute plans, maintain day-to-day operations, and coordinate across leadership.

Governance

Decision System

A two-tier voting process to ensure no decision is made impulsively.

Voting Requirements

6/9

Low Council votes required to initiate any major action

3/4

High Council approval required for confirmation

VETO

High Council retains the right to halt any decision at any stage

This system ensures:

No single person controls the kingdom
Every action is reviewed, discussed, and justified
Stability over impulsive decisions

Execution Layer

Alliance Leadership

R5s and Officers bridge the gap between kingdom command and frontline execution.

R5 Leadership

Main & Support Alliances — responsible for alliance-level execution.

  • Implement kingdom directives within alliances
  • Manage alliance activity, coordination, and discipline
  • Act as the bridge between leadership and members

Officers

Support R5s and departments in daily operations.

  • Assist with coordination, communication, and enforcement
  • Help maintain activity and organization
  • Ensure members stay aligned with kingdom goals
